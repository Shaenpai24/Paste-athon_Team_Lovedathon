Add a concept

Drag the blue port on a node's right edge onto another node to draw a prerequisite. Double-click empty canvas to create a concept. Click a node or edge, then press Delete to remove it. Import notes pulls concepts + edges from your material. Your graph auto-saves to this browser.

    Import notes

    Paste your course notes, or load a .md / .txt file. ChainForge will suggest concepts and prerequisite edges; nothing is added until you hit Apply. Everything runs locally.

    Patterns recognised: headings (# …), Prerequisites: lists, X requires Y / X depends on Y / X builds on Y, wiki-style [[Links]], and frequent capitalised phrases.

    Resolve contradiction

    Kahn's algorithm can't finish while a cycle exists. Drop any edge on the detected cycle to restore a valid total order; ChainForge will re-layer the affected descendants incrementally.
